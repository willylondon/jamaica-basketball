The Jamaica Amateur Basketball Association (JABA) Premier League is set to tip off this weekend, bringing the best club basketball action the island has to offer.

## Season Preview

The league features the top club teams from across Jamaica, competing in a format that provides consistent, high-level competition throughout the season.

## Teams to Watch

Several clubs have strengthened their rosters during the off-season, with notable player movements that could shift the balance of power in the league.

## Impact on National Team

The Premier League serves as a crucial scouting ground for the national team coaching staff. Consistent performances at this level remain the surest pathway to international selection.

## Schedule and Venues

Games will be played across multiple venues, bringing basketball to communities across the island. This decentralised approach helps grow the sport's footprint beyond the traditional Kingston stronghold.

Stay tuned for weekly recaps and highlights throughout the season.
