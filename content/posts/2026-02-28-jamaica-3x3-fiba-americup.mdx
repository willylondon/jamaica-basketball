Jamaica's 3x3 basketball programme continues to gain momentum as the team prepares for the upcoming FIBA 3x3 AmeriCup tournament.

## Growing the 3x3 Game

The 3x3 format has opened new doors for Jamaican basketball. The shorter format, faster pace, and different skill set required have created opportunities for players who might not fit the traditional 5-on-5 mould.

## Squad Selection

The coaching staff has assembled a squad that blends experience with athleticism. The half-court nature of 3x3 demands players who can score in isolation, shoot from distance, and defend in space.

## Tournament Preview

The competition will feature the strongest 3x3 nations in the Americas. Jamaica enters with realistic ambitions of progressing from the group stage and making a statement on the regional stage.

## Why 3x3 Matters for Jamaica

Beyond the immediate competition, 3x3 basketball represents a strategic opportunity for Jamaica. The format's inclusion in the Olympics and the growing global 3x3 tour circuit mean that investment in this area could yield dividends for years to come.
