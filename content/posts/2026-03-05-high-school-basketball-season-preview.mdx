The 2026 high school basketball season is upon us, and the excitement is palpable. Across the island, schoolboy and schoolgirl teams have been preparing for what promises to be a fiercely competitive campaign.

## Schoolboy Basketball

The defending champions will be looking to repeat, but they face stiff competition from several programmes that have been building steadily over the past few years.

### Top Contenders

The traditional powerhouses remain the teams to beat, with deep rosters and experienced coaching staffs. However, several emerging programmes have shown significant improvement in recent tournaments.

## Schoolgirl Basketball

The schoolgirl division continues to grow in both quality and depth. The talent pipeline is producing players who are earning attention from university programmes regionally and internationally.

## Key Players to Watch

This year's crop of talent is deep, with several players expected to make the leap to the next level. Watch for versatile guards who can score from all three levels and athletic forwards who can impact the game on both ends of the floor.

## Season Predictions

The beauty of high school basketball in Jamaica is its unpredictability. While the favourites are clear, the nature of single-elimination tournaments means that any team can make a run on any given day.
